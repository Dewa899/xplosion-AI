This application is dedicated to my parents for their persistent support and patient guidance throughout my JavaScript journey. It is also presented to the academic community at Bandung Institute of Technology as a contribution towards inspiring further advancements in JavaScript technology.
This application is part of my thesis research concerning the evaluation of explosion consequences in relation to distance for various chemical substances (AN, LPG, & H2). Its purpose is purely academic, intended as a preliminary assessment for establishing safe distances. I sincerely welcome any constructive feedback and criticism to enrich this study, both from the perspective of chemical engineering and the implementation of JavaScript technology.
